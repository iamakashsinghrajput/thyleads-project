# FINTECH VERTICAL CAMPAIGN

---

## STEP 1: Choose Your Objective

- **Select:** `Leads`
- **Goal:** `Website visits`, `Phone calls`, `Form submissions`
- **Conversion goals:** Form fill on `/contact`, Book-a-call button click

---

## STEP 2: Select Campaign Type

- **Select:** `Search`

---

## STEP 3: Campaign Name & Network Settings

- **Campaign name:** `ThyLeads — FinTech — Search — Lead Gen`
- **Networks:**
  - Google Search Network: ✅ ON
  - Google Search Partners: ❌ OFF
  - Google Display Network: ❌ OFF *(uncheck this — it wastes budget on Search campaigns)*

---

## STEP 4: Bidding

- **Bid strategy:** `Maximize Conversions`
- **Target CPA (optional):** Leave blank for the first 2 weeks. After 30+ conversions, set target CPA based on actual data (aim for $30-60 per lead depending on geo).

# WEEK 1 OPTIMIZATION CHECKLIST

After launch, check these daily for the first week:

1. **Search Terms Report** — Add irrelevant terms as negatives immediately
2. **Ad Strength** — Ensure all RSAs show "Good" or "Excellent"
3. **Quality Score** — Check keyword quality scores, pause anything below 4
4. **CTR** — Aim for 3%+ on Search. Below 2% = rewrite that ad group
5. **CPC** — Monitor cost per click, adjust bids if too high
6. **Conversion tracking** — Verify conversions are actually firing on `/contact` form
